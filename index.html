About Me

I am a Digital Game Design student focused on transforming ideas into interactive, playable experiences. I enjoy building systems from scratch, refining gameplay through iteration, and shaping visuals that strengthen the atmosphere and player experience. My work is driven by clarity, responsiveness, and a strong connection between mechanics and presentation. I approach game development as a unified process where design, development, and visual direction evolve together. By working across these areas, I aim to create experiences that are not only functional, but also cohesive and intentional. I continuously push my skills forward through hands-on creation, experimentation, and real project development.
